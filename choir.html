OUR CHOIR TEAMS

Church Choir

The Church Choir brings together enthusiastic individuals dedicated to enhancing the liturgical experience through music. The choir uplifts the parish, encouraging a deeper sense of prayer and worship during Holy Qurbana and other church celebrations.

SMYM Wellington Choir

The SMYM Wellington Choir is a vibrant group of young singers united by their love for music and faith. Comprising members of the SMYM, the choir leads worship with passion, inspiring the young generation through their commitment to glorifying God.

Catechism Students' Choir

The Catechism Students' Choir (Year 1 -12) showcases the voices of young learners as they contribute to the spiritual life of the parish. Through regular practice and engagement in church events, these students develop their musical talents and faith through the choir.

FAMILY WARDS

Strengthening our community through faith, gatherings, and shared activities

We have family units that come together with monthly rosaries, unit gatherings and fun activities to strengthen our community. We have different wards based on different locations. The wards have 2 leaders each and help bring the unit together. We have 7 family wards.

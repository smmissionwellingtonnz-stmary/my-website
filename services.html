Service Schedule

Join us for prayer and worship throughout the week

Sunday

9:30 am - Catechism

11:00 am - Holy Qurbana

5:00 pm - Rosary

5:30 pm - Holy Qurbana

**There is no evening Holy Qurbana on the first Sundays of every month**

Tuesday-Wednesday

9:30 am - Rosary

10:00 am - Holy Qurbana

Thursday-Friday

6:00 pm - Rosary

6:30 pm - Holy Qurbana

**There is no Holy Qurbana on the fourth Thursdays of every month**

Saturday

9:30 am - Rosary

10:00 am - Holy Qurbana Followed by Novena to Mother of Perpetual Help and Confession

**There is no Holy Qurbana on the second Saturdays of every month**

About Pithruvedhi

In our parish, Pithruvedhi was started in the year 2024. We had selected representatives from each ward of our parish and formed Pithruvedhi Committee. We considered all married men within our parish community will be a Pithruvedhi member by default. “Be perfect as your Heavenly Father is perfect” (Mt 5:48) is the slogan of Pithruvedhi and St. Joseph is the heavenly patron of Pithruvedhi.

Our Mission

Our mission is to unite the fathers in the parish to reflect the image of God the Father: one who loves, cares, protects, teaches, and guides the family. Our major role is to unify the community in divine faith, safeguarding the spirituality of our parish through sacrificial service and the fostering of spiritual ideologies. Pithruvedhi actively supports parish activities and are instrumental in organizing a range of fundraising events for community welfare initiatives and are key to coordinating seasonal celebrations like the Parish Feast, Christmas, and Holy Week. For our Parish, Pithruvedhi is a group of dedicated members safeguarding faith and community life.

Our Vision

The "Seeds of Hope" initiative represents our commitment to nurturing faith, fostering community growth, and spreading the message of love and compassion throughout Wellington. Like seeds planted in fertile soil, we believe that every small act of kindness, every prayer, and every moment of fellowship grows into something beautiful that transforms lives and communities.

Our Goal

Building Our Home

Our vision is to establish a dedicated church home where our community can gather, worship, and grow together in faith for generations to come.

Community Outreach

Supporting families in need through food drives, prayer groups, and pastoral care. Our mission extends beyond the church walls to embrace the wider Wellington community.

Youth Empowerment

Nurturing the next generation of faithful leaders through catechism, youth groups (SMYM), and leadership programs that build character and faith.

Faith Formation

Deepening spiritual roots through Bible study groups, prayer meetings, and sacramental preparation programs for all age groups.

Our Journey

2006

The Seed is Planted

St. Mary's Syro-Malabar Mission begins its journey in Wellington with just a few families, gathering in faith and prayer.

2015

First Roots Take Hold

The community grows to over 150 families, establishing regular Holy Qurbana and catechism classes.

2020

Blossoming Growth

SMYM youth group established, 250+ students in catechism, and dedicated chaplain appointed for the mission.

2024

Seeds of Hope Initiative

Launching expanded community outreach programs and spiritual renewal initiatives for the Wellington region.

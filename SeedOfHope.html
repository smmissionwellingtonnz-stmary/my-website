— OUR STORY —

Together, we are putting down roots

November 2025
🌱 A Vision Takes Root
In November St. Mary's Syro-Malabar Mission Wellington began an important journey towards securing a permanent spiritual home for future generations...

A dedicated feasibility study team comprising nine Parish Council members was appointed to carefully evaluate the possibility of purchasing a church property. The team conducted extensive research and analysis, focusing on key areas including affordability, long-term sustainability, family contribution requirements, ongoing operational costs, building-related considerations, and available financing options.

This marked the first significant step towards transforming a long-held dream into a realistic and achievable mission.

December 2025
🌿 Seeds of Hope Are Sown
In December 2025, under the leadership of Fr. Joseph Kunnackkattu, the "Seeds of Hope" initiative was officially introduced...

The initiative was founded on a shared vision: to establish a permanent church home where future generations can worship, learn, and grow in faith. The community embraced this vision with enthusiasm, recognizing the importance of creating a lasting spiritual legacy for the Syro-Malabar Catholic faithful.

What began as a dream soon became a united mission supported by prayer, commitment, and hope.

March 2026
🍃 United in Purpose
After months of detailed study, consultation, and planning, the feasibility study team presented its findings to the Parish Council and committee members...

The recommendations demonstrated that, with faith, commitment, and community participation, the goal of purchasing a church property was achievable. In March 2026, the proposal was presented to the wider parish community during the Pothuyogam (General Body Meeting).

Following open discussion and careful consideration, the parish community unanimously supported moving forward with the vision, marking a significant milestone in the Seeds of Hope journey.

June 2026
🌸 Growing Together for the Future
In June 2026, the Seeds of Hope fundraising campaign was officially inaugurated, launching a new chapter of collective action and generosity...

Parish ministries, family wards, youth groups, and volunteers came together to organise fundraising activities, community events, food sales, and other initiatives to support the mission. Through these efforts, the community continues to work towards securing a permanent place of worship that will serve generations to come.

Guided by faith and strengthened by unity, St. Mary's Syro-Malabar Mission Wellington continues its journey towards establishing a lasting spiritual home for the future.

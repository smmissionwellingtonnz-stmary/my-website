Our Goal

Building Our Home

Establish a dedicated church home where our community can gather, worship, and grow together in faith for generations to come.

Community Outreach

Support families through food drives, prayer groups, and pastoral care, extending beyond church walls to embrace the wider Wellington community.

Youth Empowerment

Nurture the next generation through catechism, youth groups (SMYM), and leadership programs that build character and faith.

Faith Formation

Deepen spiritual roots through Bible study groups, prayer meetings, and sacramental preparation programs for all ages.

Our Journey

2006

The Seed is Planted

St. Mary's Syro-Malabar Mission begins its journey in Wellington with just a few families, gathering in faith and prayer.

2015

First Roots Take Hold

The community grows to over 150 families, establishing regular Holy Qurbana and catechism classes.

2020

Blossoming Growth

SMYM youth group established, 250+ students in catechism, and dedicated chaplain appointed for the mission.

2024

Seeds of Hope Initiative

Launching expanded community outreach programs and spiritual renewal initiatives for the Wellington region.

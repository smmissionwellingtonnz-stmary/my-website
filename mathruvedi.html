About Mathruvedi

SM Mission Wellington Mathruvedhi launched on March 9, 2024 the day after International Women's Day. Mathruvedhi focuses on building strong families, nurturing faith, providing mutual support and empowering mothers within the church and community.

Mathruvedhi actively participates in church activities and assisting with spiritual events. A core mission is to help mothers nurture the faith of their children and pass on Christian values to future generations.

Through regular gatherings, prayer meetings, and community service, Mathruvedhi creates a supportive network where mothers can share experiences, strengthen their spiritual lives, and contribute to the growth of the parish community.

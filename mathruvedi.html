About Mathruvedi

SM Mission Wellington Mathruvedhi was launched on March 9, 2024, the day after International Women's Day. This special timing highlights our commitment to honoring and empowering women within our church and community.

Our core mission is to help mothers nurture the faith of their children and pass on Christian values to future generations.

Mathruvedhi focuses on building strong families, nurturing faith, providing mutual support, and empowering mothers within the church and community. We believe that when mothers are spiritually nourished and supported, they can better nurture the faith of their children and pass on Christian values to future generations.

Mathruvedhi actively participates in church activities and assists with spiritual events. Through regular gatherings, prayer meetings, and community service, we create a supportive network where mothers can grow in their faith and find fellowship with other women facing similar challenges and joys.

We organize various activities including family prayer sessions, Bible study groups, parenting workshops, and community outreach programs. These activities are designed to strengthen the spiritual foundation of our families while building meaningful connections within our community.

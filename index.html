Our Mission and Community

St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission is a beacon of faith, hope, and love, nurturing the spiritual and social lives of the Syro Malabar Catholic community in the Wellington, New Zealand region. We are dedicated to preserving and promoting our rich heritage and traditions for future generations.

A Warm Welcome

Dearly beloved in Christ,

It is with immense joy and gratitude that I extend a heartfelt welcome to each and every one of you to the St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission website. This digital space serves as a virtual home for our vibrant and growing community, a place where we can connect, share, and grow together in faith.

Our mission is to continue the legacy of our Syro Malabar Church, rooted in the teachings of St. Thomas the Apostle, and to serve the spiritual needs of our members. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where everyone feels part of our family, regardless of age or background.

I invite you to explore the different sections of our site to learn about our Holy Qurbana schedules, upcoming events, ministries, and the various ways you can get involved. We are a community built on prayer, fellowship, and service, and your presence enriches us all.

May the intercession of our Blessed Mother, St. Mary, guide and protect us all. With prayers and blessings,

Fr. Mathew Thazhathuveettil

Chaplain, St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission, Wellington

Welcome to St. Mary's Syro-Malabar Mission, Wellington

St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission Wellington started in 2006. One of the largest Syro Malabar faithful communities in NZ, rooted in faith and heritage.

Welcome to the St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission Wellington

Thank you for visiting St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission Wellington, New Zealand. We strive to live as a faithful Catholic community, following the vibrant Syro-Malabar traditions tracing back to St. Thomas the Apostle in India.

Currently we have 7 family wards comprising more than 350 families. Daily Holy Qurbana at 27 Trebann Street, Paparangi. Catechism classes with 250+ students and 25 teachers. Dynamic youth group SMYM.

The Community consists of migrated professionals from Kerala and different parts of India. Since 2006, growing with faith and love. We are thankful to the Catholic Archdiocese of Wellington for all support.

Fr. Joseph Kunnackattu

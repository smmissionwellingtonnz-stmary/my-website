Welcome to St. Mary's
Syro Malabar Mission

Thank you for visiting the website of St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission Wellington, New Zealand. St. Mary's Mission of the Syro-Malabar Eparchy of Melbourne strives to live as a faithful Catholic community in Wellington. Our members belong to the Syro-Malabar Major Archiepiscopal Church — one of the most vibrant Eastern Churches in the Catholic Communion — who call the greater Wellington Region home. The Syro-Malabar Church traces its origin to the preachings of St Thomas, the Apostle, in India.

The Mission currently comprises 7 family wards with more than 350 families, continuing to grow in the footsteps of St Thomas the Apostle by celebrating the Syro-Malabar Liturgy in its Synodal form. We are privileged to have daily Holy Qurbana (Holy Mass) at 27 Trebann Street, Paparangi. Our Catechism Classes run every Sunday, attended by many students and volunteer teachers, alongside our dynamic youth group, SMYM.

Our Community consists of migrated Catholic professionals from Kerala and different parts of India, present in greater Wellington since 2006. May the grace of Our Lord Jesus Christ, the love of God the Father, and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit be with us all.

Fr. Joseph Kunnackattu Chaplain — St. Mary's Syro Malabar Mission, Wellington

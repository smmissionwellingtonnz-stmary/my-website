SMYM WELLINGTON

Follow us on Instagram

Established in 2019, the Syro-Malabar Youth Movement (SMYM) Wellington aims to rejuvenate the church's youth community.

Our mission is to live and share our faith through prayer, service, and active engagement in the Church. Alongside the main youth group, there are additional groups for young families and teens, all coming together to form the SMYM Family.

Meetings take place every second Saturday of the month, offering a space for prayer, fellowship, and fun. SMYM Wellington also provides opportunities to develop leadership and teamwork skills, inspiring members to serve as role models for future generations in the community and to deepen their connection with the Church.

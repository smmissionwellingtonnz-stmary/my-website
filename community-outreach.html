Faith in Collective Action

At St. Mary's Syro-Malabar Mission Wellington, outreach is the vital heartbeat of our parish community. Guided by Christian compassion and love, we cross structural boundaries to uplift individuals, support families in crisis, preserve our shared environmental heritage, and foster connections throughout the Wellington region.

The 'Season of Giving' Initiative

Every winter and holiday season, our community unites to support local families experiencing economic hardship. Through critical alignments with regional shelters, community centers, and food banks, we generate and distribute comprehensive household resource packs, food boxes, and seasonal essentials.

This initiative ensures that the love of Christ is felt in concrete, tangible actions when families face cold winters and economic vulnerability.

Care for Our Common Home

Inspired directly by Christian structural teachings and our collective ecological responsibility, our regional youth and family groups host environmental restorative programs. From beach clean-ups across Wellington's south coast to native tree planting workshops in municipal reserves, we take active responsibility for our local environment.

Join us as we work to preserve and pass on New Zealand's unique biological beauty to the generations following us.

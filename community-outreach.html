Activity 1 · House Cleaning

House Cleaning & Rubbish Removal, Newlands

19 October 2025 1:00 PM – 6:00 PM 6 Volunteers

A patient in her late 60s, admitted to Wellington Regional Hospital, was living in severely unsanitary conditions.

  • Removed ~15 large bags of rubbish
  • Deep-cleaned interior rooms including bathroom
  • PPE kits, safety boots used indoors
Outcome & ImpactFormally acknowledged by surgical ward manager. Fundraiser raised $420.

Activity 2 · Garden & Christmas

Outdoor Tidy-Up & Christmas Decoration, Taita

07 December 2025 10 Volunteers

A single mother recovering from surgery requested help with outdoor garden and Christmas preparations.

  • Full lawn mowing across property
  • Removal of overgrown trees, garden tidy-up
  • Christmas decoration installation
Outcome & ImpactThe family enjoyed a safe, tidy outdoor space and celebrated Christmas with dignity.
